Bogdan Titomir in Stand-up

December 15, 2025 | 7:00 PM - 11:00 PM EXPO C1.2.311

Today with us — Bogdan Titomir!


Get ready for a night of energy, style, and legendary vibes! Bogdan Titomir — the iconic singer, rapper, DJ, and producer who shaped the sound of the 90s — will perform live on our stage. From his days in the hit group Car-Man to his unforgettable solo career with High Energy, Titomir has always been a pioneer of Russian hip-hop and dance-pop. Don’t miss the chance to feel the atmosphere, hear the tracks that made history, and discover why his music and charisma still capture audiences today.


One stage. One night. Infinite energy.
