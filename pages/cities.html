About Kazakhstan

Kazakhstan is the largest landlocked country in the world and a bridge between Europe and Asia. Its cities are a reflection of rich history, rapid modernization, and vibrant culture. From the futuristic skyline of Astana to the mountain views of Almaty, each city tells its own story.

Major Cities of Kazakhstan

Charyn Canyon

This majestic canyon with bright red rocks and winding trails resembles a miniature Grand Canyon, ideal for hiking and photos.

Kolsai Lakes

A cascade of three turquoise lakes in the Tian Shan mountains, surrounded by coniferous forests and alpine meadows, where you can enjoy the purest air and nature's tranquility.

Big Almaty Lake

A high-mountain lake with emerald water at the foot of the Zailiysky Alatau reflects snowy peaks, creating an unforgettable view, especially at dawn.
