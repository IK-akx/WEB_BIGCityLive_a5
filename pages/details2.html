Богдан: Retro Night

December 16, 2025 | 8:00 PM - 1:00 AM Mega Silk Way

Step back in time with Богдан Титомир for a special retro night celebrating the iconic music of the 90s. This unique event will feature all of Титомир's greatest hits performed with a full band and special visual effects that capture the spirit of the era.

Special guests from the 90s music scene will join Богдан on stage for unforgettable collaborations. The evening will also include a retro fashion show featuring styles from the decade that defined a generation.

Dress in your best 90s attire for a chance to win prizes and enjoy special themed cocktails at the bar.
